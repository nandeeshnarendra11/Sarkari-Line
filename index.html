Helpline (IVR)

Call 1800-425-0051 (toll free). Press 1 for DC Office, 2 for Passport, 3 for Ration Card. Hear wait time in Kannada / Hindi / English.

📋

Document Checklist

Each counter card shows documents needed. Tap a counter to expand checklist with checkboxes so you don't travel only to be turned away.

🔔

Wait Alerts

Tap "🔔 Alert Me" on any counter to get an SMS when wait drops below 15 min. No app required.

📶
Offline / Low Signal? Last known status cached automatically. Pull down to refresh when back online.

🏢 For Office Staff — Quick Guide

  1. Ask your District Admin for a Staff ID and 6-digit PIN
  2. Go to Admin Panel tab in navigation
  3. Enter Staff ID, Office, and 6-digit PIN to authenticate
  4. Update counter status, wait times and announcements
  5. Press "Publish Update" — goes live instantly
  6. Repeat after every major change (rush, counter closure, lunch)

Staff portal is private — citizen interface only shows public info.
